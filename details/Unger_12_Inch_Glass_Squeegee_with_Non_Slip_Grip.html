The Unger 12-Inch Glass & Surface Squeegee

Achieve a spotless, streak-free finish on your bathroom mirrors, shower doors, and car windows with this reliable glass squeegee. The durable rubber blade ensures a clean without any water spots or smudges, making it a trusted choice for everyday use and tough cleaning jobs. The non-slip handle is designed to stay secure even when your hands are wet, while the included hanger makes storage simple and convenient.

Key Features

  • High-quality rubber blade for streak-free cleaning
  • Perfect for bathroom mirrors, shower glass, and window cleaning
  • Non-slip, cushioned handle for comfort and secure grip in wet conditions
