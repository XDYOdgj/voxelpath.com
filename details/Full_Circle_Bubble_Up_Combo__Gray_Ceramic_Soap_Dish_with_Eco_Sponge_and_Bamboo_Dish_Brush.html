Full Circle Bubble Up Combo for Eco-Friendly Cleaning

This all-in-one sustainable cleaning set blends style, efficiency, and environmental care. Designed for both everyday use and eco-conscious living, it simplifies your kitchen tasks while making a positive impact.

Eco-Friendly Ceramic Soap Dish

The sleek, gray ceramic soap dish features a spring-loaded mechanism that delivers just the right amount of soap, keeping your counters clean and your soap waste-free. It's a stylish addition to your kitchen while promoting mindful usage.

Plastic-Free Cellulose Sponge

This biodegradable cellulose sponge expands when wet, providing excellent scrubbing power without the need for excessive soap. It's perfect for tackling tough grime and is better for the planet than traditional plastic sponges.

Bamboo Dish Brush

With a long, durable bamboo handle and an ergonomic grip, the dish brush is built for comfort and effectiveness. It's designed to make cleaning pots, pans, and countertops a breeze while being gentle on your surfaces.

Sustainable Materials for a Greener Kitchen

Every component of this set is crafted from natural, renewable, or recycled materials. The ceramic soap dish, bamboo brush, and cellulose sponge work together to create a functional, stylish, and eco-friendly cleaning solution that you can feel good about using.

Versatility Meets Sustainability

Whether you're washing dishes in the sink, wiping down countertops, or cleaning your kitchen surfaces, the Full Circle Bubble Up Combo is designed to fit seamlessly into your routine. Its compact and integrated design makes it easy to store and use in any space.
