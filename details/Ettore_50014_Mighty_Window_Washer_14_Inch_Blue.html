Ettore 50014 Mighty Window Washer

This powerful and user-friendly window squeegee is designed for both everyday and professional cleaning needs. Featuring a 14-inch microfiber sleeve, it delivers a streak-free finish every time while making it easier to clean larger areas efficiently. The sleeve is easily removable and machine washable, ensuring long-lasting cleanliness and convenience.

Equipped with an ergonomic handle made from durable high-impact plastic, this squeegee is comfortable to use for extended periods. Whether you're cleaning your car's windshield, home windows, or mirrors, the Ettore 50014 provides precision, reliability, and a smooth cleaning experience.

Key Features

  • 14-inch microfiber window washer for powerful cleaning
  • Removable and machine washable sleeve for easy maintenance
  • Ergonomic handle with high impact plastic for comfort and durability
  • Designed to fit all Ettore REA-CH extension poles (pole not included)
  • Versatile tool for cleaning windows, mirrors, and glass surfaces

Why Choose This Squeegee?

For those who want a durable, reusable cleaning tool, the Ettore 50014 is the perfect choice. It combines ease of use with effectiveness, making it a great addition to any cleaning routine. Its vibrant blue color adds a touch of style, and the extendable pole compatibility makes it easy to reach high or hard-to-access surfaces without straining.

Whether you're tackling seasonal cleaning tasks or looking for a reliable tool for daily use, this squeegee offers practicality and performance in one compact design.
